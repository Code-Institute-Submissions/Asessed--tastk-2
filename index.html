As we can see, majority of the moons fit into the "26 thousend to 50 thousend" bracket, while the lease fit into the "101 thousend to 500 thousend" bracket.
I think it is clear to say that as the size of these moons increases, the gravity depletes.
The bar chart above displays that the most common tempretures on these moons are quite evenly distributed between 64-65 Kelvins.
